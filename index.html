Thermal Modeling of Battery Systems on Transport Routes

Introduction

During my bachelor thesis I created a thermal simulation of the transport of lithium-ion battery cells, battery systems and electric vehicles. On this webpage I want to share an overview of my thesis and some of the results.

Methodology

  • OpenFOAM for thermal CFD simulation running on a Linux machine
  • Python for collecting historical temperature data and controlling the simulation
  • Visualisation of simulations results with Python and Paraview
